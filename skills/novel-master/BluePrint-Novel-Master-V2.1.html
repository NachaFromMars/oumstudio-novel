A. Quy Tac Viet (Forge Rules)

Bat bien. Ap dung cho MOI du an tieu thuyet.

A1 — Don Lap 400-600 Tu / Beat

Moi beat la mot don vi viet doc lap, tu hoan chinh. Toi thieu 400 tu, toi da 600 tu. TUYET DOI khong vuot. Moi chuong gom 5 beats, tong khoang 2000-3000 tu.

A2 — Khong Markdown Trong Prose

Khong dung ky tu markdown: # * ** _ --- >. Khong bullet points, khong lists, khong tables. Khong dau gach dai (—). Chi van xuoi thuan tuy voi dau cau tu nhien.

A3 — Khong Dau Vet AI

Khong dung tu AI hay dung: brilliant, stunning, delve, tapestry, journey. Khong lap pattern 3 cau lien tiep cung cau truc. Khong cau ket sao rong. Phai show, khong tell. Humanize bat buoc sau moi beat.

A4 — Giong Van Nhat Quan

Giong ke tuy project (VD: Trong Sinh = ngoi nhat "tao", hai den 70%). Giong phai nhat quan tu beat dau toi cuoi, toan bo chuong. Moi project co 1 Style Guide, tao 1 lan duy nhat (Phase 0).

A5 — Moi Nhan Vat Mot Giong Rieng

Dialogue moi nhan vat co speech pattern khac biet. Hai nhan vat noi giong nhau la FAIL (similarity > 40% = FAIL).

B. Quy Tac Tham Dinh (3-Version Beat Forge)

B1 — Moi Beat Viet 3 Phien Ban Doc Lap NEW V2.1

Moi beat → forge 3 phien ban (#1, #2, #3) viet LAN LUOT.

Moi phien ban viet DOC LAP: co the THAM CUU cac ban truoc (biet no viet gi) nhung KHONG DUNG LAM NGUYEN LIEU viet (khong copy/paste, khong dung cau tu ban truoc).

3 phien ban phai co approach/goc nhin/cach mo KHAC NHAU — KHONG PHAI 3 ban gan giong nhau.

Thu tu: Viet #1 → Viet #2 (tham cuu #1) → Viet #3 (tham cuu #1, #2)

B2 — Tham Dinh 3 Phien Ban + Chon/Mix NEW V2.1

Buoc 1: Tham dinh da dien 7 goc cho TUNG phien ban:

#GocKiem TraTrong So
1Prose QualityCau chu muot? Nhip dieu? Tu vung phong phu?20%
2ConsistencyNhat quan voi Bible? Ten, dia danh, trang thai dung?20%
3Character VoiceNhan vat noi dung giong? Hanh dong dung tinh cach?15%
4PacingBeat nhanh/cham phu hop vi tri trong chuong/arc?15%
5AI TraceCon dau vet AI? Pattern lap? Tu cam?10%
6EngagementDoc hap dan? Muon doc tiep? Hook cuoi beat?10%
7Markdown & Format0% markdown (# * ** _ --- >)? 0% dau gach dai? Prose thuan?10%

Buoc 2 — Quyet Dinh:

  • Neu 1 ban vuot troi ro rang (cach biet ≥ 0.5) → CHON ban do
  • Neu nhieu ban co highlights khac nhau → COMBINE: lay doan hay nhat tu moi ban, ghep thanh phien ban BEST
  • Khi combine: phai dam bao flow tu nhien, transitions muot, khong lo cho noi

Buoc 3 — Tham Dinh FINAL:

  • Chay lai 7 goc tham dinh tren ban FINAL
  • BAT BUOC loai bo markdown + dau vet AI trong buoc nay luon (khong doi Phase rieng)
  • Clean xong → cham diem FINAL

B3 — Diem PASS: ≥ 9/10

Ban FINAL dat 9 tro len → viet beat tiep.

Duoi 9 → viet lai 3 phien ban moi (full cycle).

Toi da 2 full cycles (6 phien ban tong) → bao anh Nang can thiep.

Diem tong = trung binh co trong so 7 goc.

I. 7 Engine Modules

Module 1 Bible Manager

Quan ly Character Bible va World Bible cho toan bo project.

Character Bible luu: Identity, Personality, Relationships, Voice, Arc, State (per chapter).

World Bible luu: Locations, Magic/Power Systems, History, Items, Factions, Rules.

Commands: bible init, bible add, bible update, bible show, bible list, bible snapshot, bible diff, bible export

File: scripts/utils/bible.mjs

Module 2 Continuity Checker

Scan chuong tim mau thuan. 30+ rules, 6 categories.

Severity: CRITICAL (fix truoc ship) | WARNING (fix truoc arc end) | NOTE (track)

Commands: check scan, check scan-all, check scan-range

File: scripts/utils/continuity.mjs

Module 3 Prose Style Analyzer

Phan tich van phong qua 7 categories: Sentence, Vocabulary, Structure, Tone, POV, AI Trace, Vietnamese-specific.

Commands: prose baseline, prose check, prose drift

File: scripts/utils/prose.mjs

Module 4 Pacing Tracker

Theo doi nhip truyen voi 6 beat types: ACTION, TENSION, REVELATION, EMOTIONAL, QUIET, TRANSITION.

Commands: pace record, pace curve, pace suggest, pace report

File: scripts/utils/pacing.mjs

Module 5 Character Voice Manager

Dam bao moi nhan vat noi giong rieng, nhat quan. Similarity < 40% giua bat ky cap nao.

Commands: voice add, voice build, voice check, voice compare, voice report

File: scripts/utils/voice.mjs

Module 6 Publisher

EPUB Premium, PDF, HTML Reader, QR Code, Audiobook. Moi arc = 1 cuon. Bundle toan series.

Commands: publish epub, publish pdf, publish html, publish qr, publish audiobook, publish all

File: scripts/utils/publish.mjs + templates/epub-premium.css

Module 7 Pipeline Orchestrator

Dieu phoi 23 skills qua 10 phases. Tu dong detect intent, check prerequisites, execute, verify gate.

Retry logic: Beat FAIL → retry 2 cycles (6 ban max). Chapter FAIL → fix + re-scan. Arc FAIL → chapter-level re-review.

File: Integrated in SKILL.md
